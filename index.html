Why Braga?

Braga, the heart of Portugal

The legend has it that Braga, one of the oldest cities in Portugal, was originally known as Bracara Augusta by the Romans. Unlike Rome, Braga's beginnings were rooted in the heart of the Minho region, known for its lush landscapes and rich history. As early as the 3rd century BC, Braga emerged as a significant cultural and religious hub in the Iberian Peninsula, often referred to as the "Portuguese Rome" due to its numerous churches and religious architecture.

You can still see traces of ancient Braga today. Beyond the modern shopping streets, tranquil squares with inviting cafes, and renowned restaurants offering delectable regional cuisine, you can explore the historic city center and discover its ancient Roman ruins, medieval churches, and beautiful gardens. From the striking Sé Cathedral to the lush Bom Jesus do Monte sanctuary, Braga offers a captivating blend of history and modernity, all set against the picturesque backdrop of rolling hills and the serene beauty of the Minho region.

My favorite cafés in Lisbon

Centésima Página

The best bookstore in Braga, Centésima Página, has an idyllic cafe in the back. You can choose to stay either inside with the books or outside in the garden.

Address:

Av. Central 118

What I like about it

This is my favorite cafe in Braga. It has easy access, since it is located in the city center, great books all around, and the best carrot pinapple cake you'll ever eat.

Nórdico Café

Also in the city center, Nordico Coffee Shop, offers a friendly service, a quiet atmosphere and a relaxed space to have a cup of coffee, eat tasty food and read a good book.

Address:

Rua do Anjo 90A

What I like about it

Perfect for coffee lovers, Nordico offers special blends and baristas that can help you make the best choice every day. The garden is cozy and the brunch is delicious.

Soul do Bem

This small café in the heart of the city has good quality coffee and great gluten free, vegetarian and vegan options for any time of the day. Pet friendly too!

Address:

Praça Conde de Agrolongo 123

What I like about it

Great friendly service and the best vegan burger in braga. If you have a sweet tooth like me, don't miss out on the cookies. Many delicious and healthy options.
